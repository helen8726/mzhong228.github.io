Overview

What is Ripple?

Ripple is a individual project I made in my start-up course at School of the Art Institute of Chicago. It is a crowdfunding platform for the creators to re-create public domain content. Readers start a topic about any public domain media and give their opinions and thoughts on that topic, then the creators may chose to re-create it or not. After few releases from the creators, fans can choose to be a patreon to support the work they like.

What are the CHALLENGES?

To create a wonderful platform for the creators and fans the communicate and support each other, this design tries to focus on those issues:

  1. How to connect creators and fans together to re-create those classic content?
  2. How to anew old content to other form of media?
  3. How to share their opinions and thoughts with each other?
  4. How to support creators in a proper way?

Design Process

I followed user-centered design throughout the design process. This includes ideation, rapid prototyping, usabilitiy testing, iteration.
